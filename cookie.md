# Cookie recipe #

## Ingredients ##
- Egg yolk
- Cornstarch
- Brown & White sugar
- Melted butter
- Chocolate chips

## Step-By-Step ##
1. Mix wet ingredients
1. Mix dry ingredients
1. Mix them
1. When its uniform, add chocolate chips inside and on top
1. Bake

### You are done! Enjoy! ###
