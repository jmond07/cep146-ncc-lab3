# Deviled Eggs Recipe #
## Ingredients ##
- Eggs
- Mayonnaise
- Dijon Mustard
- Salt & Pepper
- Hot Sauce

## Procedure ##
1. Hard boil the eggs
1. Mix the mayonnaise, the mustard, the hard boiled egg yolks and the salt with pepper
1. Put it inside the hard boiled white
1. Top up with more hoy sauce

### You are done! Enjoy! ###
